ABOUT US



      Davis Square Spa was founded in 1984 by Bamie Jrewton and his daughter John Smith. Our mission from the beginning has been to help decrease physical tension and bad energy in the Medford/Somerville area through relaxation.

Our current owner, Sandy Smith, is the daughter of co-founder John Smith. She graduated from Tufts University in 2010 with a BS in Kinesiology. Ever since graduating, Sandy has dedicated her heart and soul to the development of Davis Square Spa and the betterment of Medford/Somerville residents.









Thank you for visiting Davis Square Spa!
